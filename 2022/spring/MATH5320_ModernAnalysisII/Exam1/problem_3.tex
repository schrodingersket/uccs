Let $(X, d)$ be a complete metric space.

a.  Suppose $f:X \to X$ is a continuous function and that for some $C > 0$ and every $p, q \in X$ the following holds:
    \begin{equation*}
        d(p, q) \le C d(f(p), f(q))
    \end{equation*}

    Prove that the image $f(X)$ is complete.
